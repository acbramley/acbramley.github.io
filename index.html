Ten Thousand Stories

June 23, 2017

At VisAbility, we value greatness. Being giants in our domain, we are the leaders in our field. VisLibrary is one of VisAbility’s great legacies, carved out over 30 years.

In the last seven years alone, we’ve been providing West Australian’s with access to cutting-edge digital print materials. Last year, a massive 82, 579 DAISY Audio books were borrowed from our library. It is with great excitement that we have reached the milestone of publishing our 10,000th Audiobook!

The Apple Core Wars by Linda J Bettenay is our 10,000th Audio book. The Apple Core Wars is Linda Bettenay’s third book. The end of the three-part series, The Apple Core Wars follows the lives of characters introduced in Linda’s previous books Secrets Moths Keep and Wishes for Starlight. The books are set in Western Australia and tell the story of soldiers in the Second World War

In celebration, each week before the big milestone we had released exclusive chapters of books that are available for purchase on our website. We’re giving you the opportunity to try-before-you-buy in a whole new way. We encourage you to explore the list below, enjoying a sample of the fabulous Audio books we have on offer.

Happy listening and congratulations to Linda for her book The Apple Core Wars!
